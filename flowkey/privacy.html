Data We Process


FlowKey is an Android input method. When you enable the keyboard, it must process your keystrokes, Bopomofo composition, candidate selections, and keyboard settings on your device in order to provide keyboard functionality.


The current version stores the following data locally on your device:


  • Keyboard settings, such as theme, gesture, and preference settings.

  • Personalization data, such as candidate selection frequency and previous-word transitions, used to adjust candidate ranking.

How Data Is Used


This data is used to:


  • Display the keyboard and candidate suggestions.

  • Remember your keyboard preferences.

  • Improve candidate ranking based on local usage history.

  • Let you delete or restore selected personal frequency records.

Data Sharing


The current version has no account system, ads, analytics SDK, or cloud sync, and it does not declare internet permission. FlowKey does not send your personalized input data to the developer or to third parties.


Retention And Deletion


Keyboard settings and personalization data remain on your device until you delete available personal frequency records in the app, clear the app's data, or uninstall the app. If cloud sync or account features are added in the future, this policy will be updated before those features are released.


Security


The current version stores data in Android app-private storage and a local database. Any keyboard can process text you type; only enable input methods you trust.


Children's Privacy


FlowKey is not designed specifically for children. If the target audience or data handling changes, disclosures will be updated according to Google Play requirements.


Policy Updates


This policy may be updated for product, legal, or Google Play requirement changes. The last updated date will be changed when updates are made.
